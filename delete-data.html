Account Deletion Request

This page provides instructions on how to delete your account and associated data for Open Roots.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the app.
  2. Go to the Settings page.
  3. Scroll down to the navigation footer.
  4. Click the Delete Account button.

Data Deletion and Retention Policy

When you delete your account, the following applies:

  • Deleted Data: Your personal account information, preferences, and usage history will be permanently removed.
  • Retained Data: Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for analytical purposes and are not linked to your personal account. Additionally, backup logs may be kept for up to 30 days to ensure a complete deletion process, after which they will be permanently erased.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team at LiamOpenParty@gmail.com.
