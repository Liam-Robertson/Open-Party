Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I install the app?
    Open the App Store, search for “Open Party,” and tap “Get” to download and install the app.
  • How do I update the app?
    Go to the App Store’s “Updates” section to install the latest version.
  • I’m experiencing a bug. What should I do?
    Please report the issue by emailing us or using the in-app feedback option.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter problems while using the app, try these steps:

  1. Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed.
  2. Restart the app or your device.
  3. Check your internet connection.
  4. If the issue persists, contact our support team for further assistance.
